Integrating with a Fitness Tracker

Our app seamlessly integrates with fitness trackers like the Apple Watch to monitor physiological stress indicators such as heart rate variability and skin conductance. This allows real-time detection of stress levels, triggering personalized music therapy interventions to help manage anxiety and enhance well-being.
